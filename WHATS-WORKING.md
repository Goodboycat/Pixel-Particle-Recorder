## ⚙️ **Physics Controls (3 Working)**

### Real-time physics simulation:

1. **Gravity Slider** (-0.2 to 0.2)
   - Positive = pulls down
   - Negative = pulls up
   - Zero = floating
   - Default: 0.05
   - Tested ✅

2. **Wind Slider** (-0.2 to 0.2)
   - Positive = push right
   - Negative = push left
   - Zero = no wind
   - Default: 0
   - Tested ✅

3. **Friction Slider** (0.8 to 1.0)
   - Lower = more drag
   - Higher = smoother
   - 1.0 = no friction
   - Default: 0.99
